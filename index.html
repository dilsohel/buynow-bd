Returns & Refunds

Our Return Policy

At BuyNow BD, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • All tags and labels must be intact
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Gift cards and promotional items
  • Customized or personalized products

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer support team
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll arrange for pickup or provide return instructions
  4. Once received, we'll inspect the item
  5. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Refund Options

Refunds can be issued as:

  • Original payment method refund
  • Store credit for future purchases
  • Exchange for different size/color (subject to availability)

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping is free for defective items. For other returns, a nominal shipping fee may apply.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ▼

Placing an order is simple! Browse our products, select your size, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, fill in your details, select payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive a confirmation email with order details.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept multiple payment methods including bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and all major credit/debit cards. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.

How can I track my order? ▼

You can track your order using the Track Order section on our website. Simply enter your order ID, and you'll see real-time updates on your package location and estimated delivery time.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a 7-day return policy for defective or incorrect items. The product must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our customer support team to initiate a return.

Do you deliver to my area? ▼

Yes! We deliver to all 64 districts of Bangladesh. Delivery times vary by location - 2-3 days in Dhaka, 3-5 days in major cities, and 5-7 days in rural areas.

How do I contact customer support? ▼

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@buynowbd.com, phone at +880 1234-567890, or through the live chat feature on our website. We're available 24/7 to assist you.

Can I cancel or modify my order? ▼

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, please contact our customer support team, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Are the products authentic? ▼

Absolutely! We source all our products directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. We guarantee 100% authentic products and offer quality assurance on all items.
